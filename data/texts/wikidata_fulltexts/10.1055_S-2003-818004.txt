In the course of this phytochemical study, sevenother known compounds - calothwaitesixanthone, calozeyloxanthone,allanxanthone, isoapetalic acid 3, calolongic acid 4, apetalic acid 5 andisocalolongic acid 7 - were isolated. Their antifungal activity against the growth ofthe human pathogenic fungus Aspergillus fumigatus was then investigated.
