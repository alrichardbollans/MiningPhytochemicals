Antiinflammatory and antipyretic activities of spartidienedione isolated from PsilaspartioidesDelporte, C.Backhouse, N.Negrete, R.Gallardo, M. C.Rivera, P.The antipyretic and antiinflammatory properties of a new sesquiterpene, spartidienedione, isolatedfrom Psila spartioides (Asteraceae) were evaluated in rabbits and guinea pigs. At the dose of 25mg/kg, this substance showed antipyretic and antiinflammatory activities. Toxicity in mice wasdetermined at the dose of 5.0 g/kg.
