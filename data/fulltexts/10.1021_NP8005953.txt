Cytotoxic and Antiplasmodial Xanthones from PentadesmabutyraceaSubmitted by Emmanuel Lemoine on Wed, 12/04/2013 - 16:28Titre Cytotoxic and Antiplasmodial Xanthones from Pentadesma butyraceaType depublication Article de revueAuteurZelefack, Fabien [1], Guilet, David [2], Fabre, Nicolas [3], Bayet, Christine [4],Chevalley, Séverine [5], Ngouela, Silvère [6], Lenta, Bruno Ndjakou [7], Valentin,Alexis [8], Tsamo, Etienne [9], Dijoux-Franca, Marie-Geneviève [10]Pays Etats-UnisEditeur American Chemical SocietyVille WashingtonType Article scientifique dans une revue à comité de lectureAnnée 2009Langue AnglaisDate 2009/05/22Numéro 5Pagination 954 - 957Volume 72Titre de la revue Journal of Natural ProductsISSN 1520-6025Résumé enanglaisFour new xanthones, butyraxanthones A-D (1-4), were isolated from the stembark of Pentadesma butyracea, together with six known xanthones (5-10) and atriterpenoid (lupeol). The structures of 1-4 were established by spectroscopicmethods. Compounds 1-10 were tested in vitro for antiplasmodial activity againsta Plasmodium falciparum chloroquine-resistant strain and for cytotoxicity againsta human breast cancer cell line (MCF-7).
