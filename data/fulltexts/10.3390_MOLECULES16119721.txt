Soulamarin, a new coumarin from stem bark of calophyllum soulattri. 
 
ABSTRACT 
The extracts of the stem bark of Calophyllum soulattri gave a new pyranocoumarin, 
soulamarin (1), together with five other xanthones caloxanthone B (2), caloxanthone C (3), 
macluraxanthone (4), trapezifolixanthone (5) and brasixanthone B (6) one common 
triterpene, friedelin (7), and the steroidal triterpene stigmasterol (8). The structures of these 
compounds were established based on spectral evidence (1D and 2D NMR). 
  
Keyword: Soulamarin, Calophyllum soulattri; pyranocoumarin; Clusiaceae.
